# Fruit Store Excel Calculator

A simple Excel-based calculator created to summarise purchases in a small fruit store scenario.  
The workbook demonstrates basic business calculations, use of formulas and simple visual summaries.

## Scope of the project

1. Cost calculation  
   - price per kilogram  
   - total cost based on weight  
   - sum of all purchased products  

2. Discounts  
   - automatic discount for higher quantities  
   - recalculated totals after discount  

3. Packaging suggestion  
   - estimated number of bags needed based on total weight  

4. Chart  
   - cost distribution per product  
   - simple visual breakdown of spending  

## Purpose

The project shows the ability to prepare small analytical tools in Excel, perform cost calculations and present results in a clear format.
